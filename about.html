Our Project

Hawlucha Helper is a website that allows users to search for information about different Pokemon. Users can search for Pokemon by name or by their index number in the Pokedex. Our website returns said Pokemon's name, Pokedex number, and habitat. Our website retrieves this data from PokeAPI.
